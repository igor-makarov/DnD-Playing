# Character Build Plan: CHA-First Rogue

## Overview
- **Class:** Rogue, Level 1
- **Species:** Human (PHB 2024 / XPHB)
- **Ruleset:** PHB 2024 (XPHB source)
- **Concept:** Charismatic rogue focusing on social manipulation and magic

## Ability Scores

### Base Array (Epic Array)
- CHA: 18
- DEX: 16
- CON: 15
- STR: 13
- INT: 11
- WIS: 9 (dump stat)

### With Background ASIs (+2 CHA / +1 CON)
- **CHA: 20 (+5)** - Primary stat, spell DC 13, spell attack +5
- **DEX: 16 (+3)** - AC, attacks, Stealth
- **CON: 16 (+3)** - HP (11 at level 1: 8 + 3)
- **STR: 13 (+1)** - Athletics if needed
- **INT: 11 (+0)** - Investigation
- **WIS: 9 (-1)** - Perception/Insight weakness

## Background: Custom

### Background Components
- **Abilities:** DEX, CON, CHA (player chooses +2/+1 or +1/+1/+1 distribution)
  - **Chosen:** +2 CHA / +1 CON
- **Origin Feat:** Magic Initiate (Wizard)
  - Cantrips: True Strike, Toll the Dead
  - 1st level spell: Mage Armor (1/day)
  - Spellcasting ability: CHA (INT or WIS also allowed, but CHA chosen)
- **Skill Proficiencies (2):** Performance, Athletics
- **Tool Proficiency (1):** Alchemist's Supplies
- **Equipment (50 GP):** Fine Clothes (15 GP), Traveler's Clothes (2 GP), portion of adventure gear and weapons below

### Background Name/Concept
**Secret Society Operative** - Character worked for a clandestine organization

## Species: Human (XPHB)

### Human Traits
- **Size:** Medium (4-7 feet tall)
- **Speed:** 30 ft
- **Resourceful:** Gain Heroic Inspiration after each Long Rest
- **Skillful:** +1 skill proficiency of choice → **Acrobatics**
- **Versatile:** +1 Origin feat → **Alert**

### Alert Feat (Origin, from Human)
- **Initiative Proficiency:** Add proficiency bonus to Initiative rolls (+2 at level 1)
- **Initiative Swap:** After rolling Initiative, can swap with willing ally (if neither Incapacitated)

## Class: Rogue Level 1

### Hit Points
- **Hit Die:** 1d8
- **HP at Level 1:** 8 + CON modifier = 8 + 3 = **11 HP**

### Proficiencies
- **Armor:** Light armor
- **Weapons:** Simple weapons + Martial weapons with Finesse or Light property
- **Tools:** Thieves' Tools
- **Saving Throws:** Dexterity, Intelligence
- **Skills:** Choose 4 from: Acrobatics, Athletics, Deception, Insight, Intimidation, Investigation, Perception, Persuasion, Sleight of Hand, Stealth
  - **Chosen:** Deception, Intimidation, Persuasion, Stealth

### Level 1 Class Features

#### Expertise
- Choose 2 skill proficiencies to gain Expertise (double proficiency bonus)
- **Chosen:** Persuasion, Stealth

#### Sneak Attack (1d6)
- Once per turn, deal extra 1d6 damage when:
  - You have Advantage on attack roll with Finesse or Ranged weapon, OR
  - An ally is within 5 feet of target (you don't have Disadvantage, ally not Incapacitated)

#### Thieves' Cant
- Know Thieves' Cant secret language
- Choose 1 additional language → *TO BE DECIDED*

#### Weapon Mastery
- Use mastery properties of 2 Simple or Martial weapons you're proficient with
- Can change choices after Long Rest
- **Chosen:** Shortsword (Vex), Dagger (Nick)

## Weapons & Combat

### Primary Weapons
- **Melee:** Shortsword (1d6 piercing, Finesse, Light, Vex mastery)
- **Melee/Off-hand:** Dagger (1d4 piercing, Finesse, Light, Nick mastery)
- **Ranged:** Dart (1d4 piercing, Finesse, Thrown, Vex mastery)
- **Ranged:** Shortbow (1d6 piercing, Vex mastery)

### Combat Stats
- **AC:** 13 (studded leather: 12 + 3 DEX)
- **Initiative:** +3 DEX + 2 proficiency (Alert) = **+5**
- **Attack Bonus:** +3 DEX + 2 proficiency = **+5 to hit**
- **Sneak Attack:** +1d6 damage (once per turn)

### Weapon Mastery
- **Shortsword:** Vex (Advantage on next attack vs same target)
- **Dagger:** Nick (Light weapon off-hand attack as part of Attack action)
