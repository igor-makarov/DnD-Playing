# Ability Modifier Scaling Abilities (XPHB)

This reference lists all XPHB abilities that scale with specific Ability Modifiers, excluding save DC calculations.

## Species/Races

### Goliath

- **Giant's Endurance**: Reduce damage by 1d12 + **CON modifier**

## Feats

### Boon of Fortitude

- Gain +**CON modifier** HP when healing (once per turn)

### Tavern Brawler

- Unarmed Strike: 1d4 + **STR modifier** Bludgeoning damage

### Unarmed Fighting

- Unarmed Strike: 1d6 (or 1d8) + **STR modifier** Bludgeoning damage

## Classes

### Barbarian

- **Unarmored Defense** (1): AC = 10 + **DEX** + **CON modifier**

### Bard

- **Bardic Inspiration** (1): Usable **CHA modifier** times per long rest
- **Dance - Bardic Damage** (3): Unarmed Strike = Bardic Die + **DEX modifier**
- **Dance - Unarmored Defense** (3): AC = 10 + **DEX** + **CHA modifier**
- **Glamour - Mantle of Inspiration** (3): Affect **CHA modifier** creatures, gain 2× Bardic Die temp HP

### Cleric

- **Thaumaturge** (1): +**WIS modifier** to Arcana/Religion checks
- **Divine Spark** (2): Heal or damage = 1d8 + **WIS modifier**
- **Sear Undead** (5): Undead take **WIS modifier** d8s Radiant damage
- **Potent Spellcasting** (7): Add **WIS modifier** to cantrip damage
- **Improved Blessed Strikes** (14): Grant 2× **WIS modifier** temp HP

#### Light Domain

- **Warding Flare** (3): Usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest
- **Improved Warding Flare** (6): Grant 2d6 + **WIS modifier** temp HP
- **Corona of Light** (17): Usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest

#### War Domain

- **War Priest** (3): Bonus attack **WIS modifier** times per short/long rest

### Druid

- **Magician** (1): +**WIS modifier** to Arcana/Nature checks
- **Potent Spellcasting** (7): Add **WIS modifier** to cantrip damage

#### Circle of the Moon

- **Circle Forms** (3): AC = 13 + **WIS modifier** (if higher)
- **Improved Circle Forms** (6): Add **WIS modifier** to CON saves
- **Moonlight Step** (10): Usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest

#### Circle of Stars

- **Star Map** (3): Cast Guiding Bolt **WIS modifier** times per long rest
- **Archer** (3): Ranged attack deals 1d8 + **WIS modifier** Radiant
- **Chalice** (3): Heal 1d8 + **WIS modifier** HP
- **Cosmic Omen** (6): Usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest

### Fighter

- **Champion - Survivor** (18): Regain 5 + **CON modifier** HP per turn when bloodied

#### Psi Warrior

- **Protective Field** (3): Reduce damage by die + **INT modifier**
- **Psionic Strike** (3): Deal die + **INT modifier** Force damage
- **Bulwark of Force** (15): Grant Half Cover to **INT modifier** creatures

### Monk

- **Unarmored Defense** (1): AC = 10 + **DEX** + **WIS modifier**
- **Deflect Attacks** (3): Reduce damage by 1d10 + **DEX modifier** + level
- **Deflect Attacks** (3): Redirect damage = 2× Martial Die + **DEX modifier**

#### Way of Mercy

- **Hand of Harm** (3): Extra Martial Die + **WIS modifier** Necrotic
- **Hand of Healing** (3): Heal Martial Die + **WIS modifier** HP
- **Flurry of Healing and Harm** (11): Usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest
- **Hand of Ultimate Mercy** (17): Revive with 4d10 + **WIS modifier** HP

#### Way of the Open Hand

- **Wholeness of Body** (6): Heal Martial Die + **WIS modifier** HP
- **Wholeness of Body** (6): Usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest

### Paladin

- **Aura of Protection** (6): +**CHA modifier** to saves (minimum +1)
- **Abjure Foes** (9): Target **CHA modifier** creatures

#### Oath of Devotion

- **Sacred Weapon** (3): +**CHA modifier** to attack rolls
- **Holy Nimbus** (20): Deal **CHA modifier** + PB Radiant per turn

#### Oath of Glory

- **Glorious Defense** (15): +**CHA modifier** AC bonus (minimum +1)
- **Glorious Defense** (15): Usable **CHA modifier** times per long rest

### Ranger

- **Tireless** (10): 1d8 + **WIS modifier** temp HP; usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest
- **Nature's Veil** (14): Usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest

#### Fey Wanderer

- **Otherworldly Glamour** (3): +**WIS modifier** to CHA checks
- **Misty Wanderer** (15): Cast **WIS modifier** times per long rest

#### Gloom Stalker

- **Dread Ambusher** (3): Usable **WIS modifier** times per long rest
- **Dread Ambusher** (3): +**WIS modifier** to Initiative

### Sorcerer

#### Aberrant Mind

- **Telepathic Speech** (3): Range = **CHA modifier** miles

#### Clockwork Soul

- **Restore Balance** (3): Usable **CHA modifier** times per long rest

#### Draconic Bloodline

- **Draconic Resilience** (3): AC = 10 + **DEX** + **CHA modifier**
- **Elemental Affinity** (6): +**CHA modifier** to spell damage

### Warlock

#### Archfey Patron

- **Steps of the Fey** (3): Cast Misty Step **CHA modifier** times per long rest

#### Celestial Patron

- **Healing Light** (3): Spend up to **CHA modifier** dice at once
- **Radiant Soul** (6): +**CHA modifier** to spell damage once per turn
- **Celestial Resilience** (10): Gain level + **CHA modifier** temp HP
- **Searing Vengeance** (14): Deal 2d8 + **CHA modifier** Radiant

#### Fiend Patron

- **Dark One's Blessing** (3): Gain **CHA modifier** + level temp HP
- **Dark One's Own Luck** (6): Usable **CHA modifier** times per long rest

#### Great Old One Patron

- **Awakened Mind** (3): Range = **CHA modifier** miles
- **Create Thrall** (14): Aberration gets level + **CHA modifier** temp HP

### Wizard

#### Abjuration School

- **Arcane Ward** (3): Ward HP = 2× level + **INT modifier**

#### Evocation School

- **Empowered Evocation** (10): Add **INT modifier** to Evocation spell damage

## Patterns by Ability

### Strength (STR)

**Damage scaling**: Tavern Brawler, Unarmed Fighting

### Dexterity (DEX)

**AC formulas, damage**: Barbarian/Monk Unarmored Defense, Bard Dance, Monk deflection

### Constitution (CON)

**Damage reduction, HP regeneration**: Goliath, Barbarian AC, Fighter Survivor, Boon of Fortitude

### Intelligence (INT)

**Damage/damage reduction, ward HP**: Psi Warrior, Abjurer, Evoker

### Wisdom (WIS)

**Most versatile** - healing, damage, temp HP, number of uses, skill bonuses, AC

- Used by: Cleric, Druid, Monk, Ranger

### Charisma (CHA)

**Number of uses, temp HP, range, saves bonus, damage, AC**

- Used by: Bard, Paladin, Sorcerer, Warlock
