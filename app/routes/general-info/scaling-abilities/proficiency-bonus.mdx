# Proficiency Bonus Scaling Abilities (XPHB)

This reference lists all XPHB abilities that scale directly with Proficiency Bonus, excluding those that only use PB for save DC calculations.

## Species/Races

### Aasimar

- **Healing Hands**: Touch heals PB×d4 HP (1/long rest)
- **Transformation damage**: +PB damage once per turn (Necrotic/Radiant)
- **Inner Radiance**: PB Radiant damage to creatures within 10ft at end of each turn

### Dragonborn

- **Breath Weapon**: Usable **PB times** per long rest

### Dwarf

- Bonus Action ability usable **PB times** per long rest

### Gnome

- **Speak with Animals**: Cast **PB times** per long rest

### Goliath

- **Giant Ancestry benefits**: Usable **PB times** per long rest

### Orc

- **Adrenaline Rush**: Gain **PB temp HP**; usable **PB times** per short/long rest

## Feats

### Combat & Defense

- **Alert**: Add **PB to Initiative** rolls
- **Defensive Duelist**: Add **PB to AC** as reaction
- **Great Weapon Master**: Extra damage = **PB** with Heavy weapons
- **Heavy Armor Master**: Reduce B/P/S damage by **PB**
- **Interception**: Reduce damage by 1d10 + **PB**
- **Shield Master**: Bash target (uses PB for save DC only)

### Support & Utility

- **Chef**: Make food for **4+PB creatures**; make **PB treats** that grant **PB temp HP**
- **Healer**: Healing = Hit Die + **PB**
- **Lucky**: **PB Luck Points** per long rest
- **Musician**: Grant Heroic Inspiration to **PB allies** after rest

### Magic & Ritual

- **Poisoner**: Create **PB poison doses**
- **Ritual Caster**: Know **PB level 1 ritual spells**

## Class Features

### Bard

- **Jack of All Trades** (Level 2): Add **half PB** to non-proficient ability checks

### Druid

- **Wild Shape** (Level 2): Use **your PB** for proficiencies while in beast form

### Paladin - Subclasses

- **Devotion - Holy Nimbus** (Level 20): **CHA + PB Radiant damage** per turn to enemies in aura

### Rogue - Subclasses

- **Soulknife - Psychic Whispers** (Level 3): Connect with **PB creatures** telepathically

### Wizard - Subclasses

- **Abjurer - Spell Breaker** (Level 10): Add **PB to Dispel Magic** ability check

## Scaling Patterns

### Number of Uses = PB

Dragonborn Breath, Dwarf, Gnome, Goliath, Orc, Lucky, Poisoner

### Direct Damage/Healing = PB

Aasimar, Great Weapon Master, Paladin Holy Nimbus

### Damage Reduction = PB

Heavy Armor Master, Interception (1d10 + PB)

### Bonus to Rolls/AC = PB

Alert (Initiative), Defensive Duelist (AC), Jack of All Trades (½PB), Spell Breaker

### Resource Amounts = PB

Chef (treats/creatures), Musician (allies), Ritual Caster (spells), Soulknife (targets)

### Temporary HP = PB

Orc Adrenaline Rush, Chef treats
